AlgebraicRewriting.jl

Although literate documentation is forthcoming, at present one can get a sense of AlgebraicRewriting by checking out the following:

  • full demo: a small demonstration of most of the major features of AlgebraicRewriting.
  • Lotka Volterra example: a model based on NetLogo's wolf-sheep predation agent-based model demo.
  • Game of Life example: an implementation of Conway's game of life on an arbitrary graph (with the grid-structure as a special case).
